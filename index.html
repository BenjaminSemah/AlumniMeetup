GHANA MICRONAUTS MEETUP 2022

"Hello! Micronauts"

A day of interaction with some of the world's most promising software devs. Come learn from developers, have fun and network. You wouldn't want to miss this.

2022.10.29(SAT)

@International DEV. Center, Accra, Ghana

Activities Include

  • Lecture

    Listen to inspiring talks by renowned Software developers from various countries.

  • Interviews

    Mock interviews with recruiters from reputable organizations.

  • Workshops

    Workshops on various topics such as how to craft your resume.

  • Networking

    Network with other Micronauts from around the world.

  • Tour

    Explore the beautiful city of Accra, Ghana's capital.
