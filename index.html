GHANA MICRONAUTS MEETUP 2022

"Hello! Micronauts"

A day of interaction with some of the world's most promising software devs. Come learn from developers, have fun and network. You wouldn't want to miss this.

2022.10.29(SAT)

@International DEV. Center, Accra, Ghana

Activities Includes

  • Lecture

    Listen to inspiring messages from renowned Software developers from various countries. Be inspired and take action to inspire others.

  • Interviews

    Speed interview sessions with recruiters from reputable organizations. Come prepared to take advantage of this opportunity. Your first or next developer role might just be waiting for you.

  • Workshops

    Workshops on various topics such as how to craft your resume, how to prepare for a developer job interview, personal branding for software developers, building an online audience among others.

  • Networking

    Connect and network with other Micronauts from around the world. And also with senior developers and recruiters.

  • Tour

    Explore the beautiful city of Accra. Experience the sights and sounds of one of Africa's most cosmopolitan cities.
